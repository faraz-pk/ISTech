Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

Simply fill in the contact form above or call us directly. Our admissions team will guide you through the enrollment process and provide schedule options.

Are courses available online or in-person?

We offer both online (via Zoom/LMS) and in-person classes at our Rawalpindi campus. You can choose the format that suits your schedule best.

Do I get a certificate after completing a course?

Yes, all ISTech courses include a professional certificate of completion. Certain courses also prepare you for industry certifications like Microsoft or PMI.

How long does an ERP implementation take?

Implementation timelines vary by scope — small systems can go live in 4–6 weeks, while full enterprise deployments typically take 3–6 months depending on complexity.

Do you offer group or corporate training?

Absolutely. We offer customized corporate training packages for teams and organizations. Contact us to discuss your requirements and we'll design a tailored program.

What payment options are available?

We accept full payment, installment plans, and corporate invoicing. We also occasionally offer early-bird discounts and scholarships for outstanding students.
