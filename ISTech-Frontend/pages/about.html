We're on a mission to make professional, industry-grade education and enterprise technology accessible to everyone. Empowering individuals and businesses to achieve excellence through innovation, expertise, and trust.

Who We Are

Bridging Education
& Technology

ISTech is a professional education and technology solutions company based in Rawalpindi, Pakistan. We combine deep industry expertise with modern learning methodologies to empower professionals and transform businesses through industry-led courses and enterprise solutions.

Founded by industry veterans and educators, ISTech bridges the critical gap between classroom theory and real-world application. We excel in professional training — Power BI, Web Development, Accounting Fundamentals, SAP, IFRS, AWS, and Interview Skills — while delivering enterprise-grade ERP systems, QuickBooks implementations, advanced analytics, and strategic deployment solutions. Our success is measured by the success of our students and clients.
