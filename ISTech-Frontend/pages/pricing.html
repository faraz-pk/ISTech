Pricing FAQs

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a 7-day free preview on all Pro and Elite plans. No credit card required to start your trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfer, JazzCash, EasyPaisa, and credit/debit cards. Installment plans are available on request.

Do individual course fees include the certificate?

Yes — every course fee includes a digital certificate of completion. Physical certificates are available for a small printing fee.
